Evidence-Based Approaches

I integrate multiple therapeutic modalities, selected based on your unique needs and preferences. Here's an overview of the approaches I specialize in.

Primary Modality

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

CBT helps you identify patterns in thinking and behavior that contribute to your distress, then gently shift those patterns toward greater wellbeing.

  • Addresses anxiety, depression, phobias
  • Teaches practical coping skills
  • Empowers you with tools for life
Trauma Specialty

Trauma-Informed Therapy

Healing from trauma requires gentleness and power. This approach recognizes how trauma affects your body and mind, and honors your resilience every step of the journey.

  • Creates safety and control
  • Regulates nervous system
  • Reclaims agency and power
Narrative Approach

Narrative Therapy

Your story is powerful. Narrative therapy helps you re-author your story, separate your identity from the problem, and step into the person you want to become.

  • Honors your unique story
  • Externalize the problem
  • Discover resilience & strength

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? Here are answers to common concerns that help people decide if therapy is right for them.

What happens in a first session?

+

Your first session is about building comfort and understanding. We'll start slowly. I'll listen to what brings you in, ask clarifying questions to understand your world, and explain how I work. You're always in control of the pace. Many people feel nervous before their first session‚Äîthat's completely normal. We'll go at your speed.

How long is one session?

+

Standard sessions are 50 minutes. This allows sufficient time for deep work and meaningful conversation. Some clients prefer 90-minute sessions for intensive work or initial assessments. We can discuss what feels right for your needs and goals.

Is everything I share confidential?

+

Yes, with a few legal exceptions. I'm legally required to break confidentiality if there's risk of serious harm to yourself or others, abuse of minors or elderly persons, or court orders. We'll discuss these limits in detail during your first session.

Do you specialize in my specific issue?

+

I specialize in anxiety, depression, trauma, relationship issues, life transitions, burnout, identity exploration, and grief. I also work with adolescents and young adults navigating complex developmental challenges. If you have a concern not listed, reach out‚ÄîI may still be able to help or refer you to a specialist who can.

Do you offer online sessions?

+

Yes! Online therapy is equally effective. Secure, private video sessions allow you to join from home with a click. Research shows online therapy has the same positive outcomes as in-person therapy. Some people prefer online for privacy or convenience; others prefer in-person. Your choice.

What if therapy doesn't work for us?

+

Good question. If you feel we're not connecting or it's not the right fit, we can discuss whether a different therapist might be better. My goal is YOUR wellbeing, not keeping you as a client. Therapy works best when there's genuine trust and connection. If that's not there, let me know.

Is this confidential information safe online?

+

Yes. All online sessions use HIPAA-compliant, encrypted video platforms. Your data is never shared or sold. I use secure email and messaging systems. Your privacy and security are non-negotiable. I take every precaution to protect your information.
